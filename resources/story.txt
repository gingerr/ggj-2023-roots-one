The root of all evil

The intergalactiv brain has choosen you for an important mission:

The natural order is danger. On the planet "SquareRoot" the 
leaders have choosen to rise a rebellion. For hundreds of years 
there has been peace  according to the agreement to only sent 
roots into the universe that compute to natural numbers. This made
sure, that all the citizens of the universe preserve their sanitiy.

You are now the last defense between madness and sanity. With your
trustworthy battleship you have to shoot down all mad roots and make 
sure they don't reach the intergalactic gateway.
